\# PropertySignalHQ



PropertySignalHQ is a real estate opportunity discovery platform designed to help investors find undervalued or motivated-seller properties using signal-based analysis.



Instead of manually searching listings, PropertySignalHQ analyzes property data and surfaces high-potential investment opportunities automatically.



\---



\## Features



\### Opportunity Discovery

\- Signal scoring engine

\- Daily opportunities feed

\- Hot deal detection

\- Market heatmap overview



\### Property Analysis

\- Property detail pages

\- Price history visualization

\- Deal calculator

\- Opportunity explanation engine



\### Investor Workflow

\- Favorites tracking

\- Investor notes

\- Opportunity alerts

\- Deal pipeline tracking



\### Deal Management

\- Status tracking (New Lead → Closed)

\- Exportable deal reports

\- CSV export of leads
